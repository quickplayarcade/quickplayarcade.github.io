Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

QuickPlay Arcade (“we”, “our”, “us”) is an ad-supported mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the app and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not require an account and we do not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number in order to use the app.

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide and measure ads. Depending on your device settings and your consent choices, this may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., the Advertising Identifier / IDFA on iOS, when available)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (such as ad impressions and clicks)
  • Diagnostic and performance data (to help ensure ads are delivered correctly)

2. Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

If you allow tracking on iOS (see section 3), AdMob may use your Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for ad personalization and measurement. If you do not allow tracking, ads may still be shown, but they may be less relevant (non-personalized).

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple requires your permission for tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies. If our app requests this permission, you can choose Allow or Ask App Not to Track. You can change your choice at any time in your device settings:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

4. Third-Party Services

Advertising is provided by Google AdMob. Google may process information as described in Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Children’s Privacy

QuickPlay Arcade is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Your Choices

  • You can manage iOS tracking permission in your device settings (see section 3).
  • You can reset your Advertising Identifier and limit ad personalization via your device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: quickplayarcade@outlook.com
