FAQ

Ads not loading?

Check your internet connection and try again later. If you chose “Ask App Not to Track,” ads can still load, but they may be less personalized.

Is my progress saved?

Game progress and high scores are saved locally on your device. Deleting the app may remove local data.

App crashed or feels slow?

Please restart the app and make sure you’re on the latest version. If it continues, email us details so we can fix it.
